Kia

1- Launch Date: The Kia Seltos was launched in India in August 2019.

2-Variants: The Seltos is available in multiple variants, offering different features and engine options. Some of the variants include Tech Line and GT Line.

3-Engine Options: The Seltos offers a range of petrol and diesel engines, including 1.5-liter petrol, 1.4-liter turbocharged petrol, and 1.5-liter diesel engines. Transmission options include manual and automatic transmissions.

4-Features: The Seltos is known for its feature-packed interior, with a touchscreen infotainment system, UVO connected car technology, multiple airbags, ABS with EBD, panoramic sunroof, ventilated seats, and more, depending on the variant.

5-Design: Kia Seltos boasts a modern and stylish design with a distinctive tiger-nose grille, LED headlamps, and sharp character lines, giving it a bold and contemporary look.

6-Dimensions: The SUV has dimensions that fall within the compact SUV segment, making it suitable for urban as well as highway driving.

7-Safety: It comes equipped with safety features such as airbags, ABS with EBD, traction control, electronic stability control, hill assist, and more, ensuring a safe driving experience.

8-Price Range: The Kia Seltos is positioned competitively in the Indian market, with different variants catering to various price points
